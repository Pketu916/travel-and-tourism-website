Ayodhya Ram Mandir

Description

Ayodhya Ram Mandir is a Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Rama, located in Ayodhya, Uttar Pradesh, India. It is believed to be the birthplace of Lord Rama and is one of the holiest sites in Hinduism. The temple complex is planned to be one of the largest Hindu temples in the world.
